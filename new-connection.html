Electrical Permit (2 copies) Required

Certificate of Final Electrical Inspection (2 copies) Required
Also from the Office of Building Officials at your Local Government Unit.

Electrical Plan signed by a Professional Electrical Engineer (2 copies) Required
Obtain from your electrician, architect, or electrical engineer.

Service Drop Wire Affidavit of Consent Required
